# Contents

A map of what's in this repository, organized by domain. See the [root README](./README.md) for the project's purpose and background.

## Sanskrit (Samskrit)
Grammar resources, classical texts, academic papers, magazines, and course materials for learning Sanskrit.
- [Awesome Samskrit](./Samskrit/Awesome_samskrit.md): curated list of Sanskrit learning resources
- `Samskrit/Grammar/`: grammar references
- `Samskrit/Classics/`: classical Sanskrit texts
- `Samskrit/Papers/`: academic papers
- `Samskrit/Magazines/`: magazine scans
- `Samskrit/Courses/`: personal notes, exercises, and reference material from Sanskrit courses taken by the maintainer
- `Samskrit/LaTeX/`: shared LaTeX style/preamble files used across the repo's Beamer decks

## Yoga Philosophy (YogaShastra)
- [YogaShastra overview](./YogaShastra/README.md): curated resources on yoga philosophy, foundational texts, instructional guides, academic research, books, and courses
- [YogaSutra](./YogaShastra/YogaSutra/README.md): materials on Patanjali's Yoga Sutras
  - [YogaSutraTrees](./YogaShastra/YogaSutra/YogaSutraTrees/README.md): a machine-readable knowledge graph of the Yoga Sutras (`graph.json`), with Devanagari text, transliteration, word-by-word analysis, multiple English translations, and commentaries
- [YogaNidra](./YogaShastra/YogaNidra/README.md): Yoga Nidra resources
- `YogaShastra/ArticlesPapers/`, `Aurobindo/`, `Certification/`, `Iyengar/`, `Meditation/`, `MHThaker/`, `NIOSYoga/`, `RakeshDeshpande/`, `RamKishore/`, `Sadhguru/`, `Shrimath/`, `Yogalaya/`, `YogicJourney/`: teacher- and topic-specific reference material

## LaTeX Presentations and Documents
- [LaTeX build system and file guide](./LaTeX/README.md): compiled Beamer presentations and cheat sheets for the YogaSutra seminar series, YogaShastra workshop, Arthashastra, and other domains, plus the batch scripts used to build them

## Indian Knowledge Systems Coursework (IKS)
Master's-level coursework (Bhishma MCKS program) and related reference material.
- [Arthashastra](./IKS/Arthashastra/Awesome_arthashastra.md): Kautilya's text on governance and geopolitics
- `IKS/Ayurveda/`: traditional medicine reference material
- `IKS/Bhishma_MCKS/`: coursework from the Bhishma MCKS program
- `IKS/IndianKnowledgeSystems/`: general IKS reference material

## Dasbodh
- [Dasbodh overview](./Dasbodh/README.md): study of Samarth Ramdas Swami's Dasbodh via email-based learning, with assignments and references

## Admin
Personal certificates, mindmap notes, and exam records related to the maintainer's own coursework. Not a content domain in itself; kept for reference.
